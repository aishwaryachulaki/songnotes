Getting started
Keepsake is a Chrome extension that lets you attach personal notes to specific moments in a song. When your recipient listens to that track, your note appears on their screen at the exact second you chose — like a handwritten letter tucked inside the music.
Keepsake works on the Spotify web player in Chrome. You'll need an active Spotify account — free or premium — to listen and write notes. The recipient also needs a Spotify account to receive them while listening.
Yes — they'll need the Chrome extension to receive notes while listening. If they don't have it installed, the share page will prompt them. It only takes a moment to set up.
How it works
A timestamp is the exact moment in a song where your note appears. You can type one manually (e.g. 1:24) or hit the "Now" button while the song is playing to capture the current position.
Yes. You can write notes across several songs and bundle them into one share link — the recipient gets all of them delivered across their listening session.
Yes. If the listener rewinds past a timestamp, the note fires again. The experience is designed to feel like rediscovering something each time.
You can delete notes from the extension before sharing. Once a share link has been sent and imported by the recipient, the notes are locked — so take your time before you share.
Privacy
Yes. Note content is intentionally hidden on the share page — the recipient sees which songs to listen to, but your actual words only appear at the right moment while the song plays. No one else can read them.
Notes are stored securely and only used to deliver them to your recipient. We don't read, sell, or share your note content with anyone.
Pricing

Compatibility
Not yet — Keepsake is a Chrome extension and works on desktop browsers only. Mobile support is something we're actively exploring.

Troubleshooting
Make sure the extension is active and you're on the Spotify web player in Chrome. Check that the share was imported correctly — you can do this from the extension popup. If it still doesn't work, try refreshing the Spotify tab.
