Welcome to Keepsake ✦

Keepsake lets you pin little notes to moments in a song and send them to someone. Here's how to make your first one — it takes about a minute.

1
Pin Keepsake to your toolbar
Click the puzzle-piece ⊕ Extensions icon at the top-right of your browser, find Keepsake, and hit the pin so it's always one click away.
This is Keepsake — pin it, then click it
any time from your toolbar.
2
Sign in
Create your account (or sign in) so your keepsakes are saved to you.
Sign in →
3
Open the Spotify Web Player & play any song
Keepsake works right on top of Spotify in your browser. Open it and press play on anything you like.
Open Spotify Web Player →
4
Click the Keepsake ✦ icon & follow along
With a song playing, click the Keepsake icon in your toolbar to open the side panel. A gentle guided tour pops up as the song plays and walks you through writing and sharing your first keepsake.
