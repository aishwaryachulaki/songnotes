Keepsake works on the Spotify web player in Chrome and other Chromium-based browsers (Brave, Edge, Opera and more). You'll need an active Spotify account, either free or premium.
Yes — the recipient needs the extension installed and an account to receive your notes while listening. If they don't have either, the share page will prompt them to get set up. You just send the link; the rest takes care of itself.
Yes. Note content is intentionally hidden on the share page. The recipient sees which songs to listen to, but your actual words only appear at the right moment while the song plays. Your notes are also end-to-end encrypted: the key never touches our servers, so only you and your recipient can read them.
