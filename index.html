Keepsake works on the Spotify web player in Chrome. You'll need an active Spotify account — free or premium. No account is needed to visit a share page, but the recipient needs the extension installed to receive notes while listening.
Yes — they'll need the Chrome extension to receive notes while listening. If they don't have it, the share page will prompt them to install it. It only takes a moment.
Yes. Note content is intentionally hidden on the share page — the recipient sees which songs to listen to, but your actual words only appear at the right moment while the song plays. No one else can read them.
You get 3 free letters when you sign up. After that, you can buy credit packs starting at ₹150, or go lifetime for unlimited notes with a one-time purchase.
Not yet — Keepsake is a Chrome extension and works on desktop browsers only. Mobile support is something we're actively exploring.
You can delete notes from the extension before sharing. Once a share link has been sent and imported by the recipient, the notes are locked — so take your time before you share.
